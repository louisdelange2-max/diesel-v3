Intercompany Diesel Transfer

This transfers diesel ownership between companies inside the same bowser. Physical bowser liters do not change and no machine fill is created.

Company Settlement

Choose Money paid back when the debtor company settles in cash and no diesel moves. Choose Diesel paid back from available balance when the debtor company gives diesel back from its available balance in the selected bowser.

Export Reports

Export reports use the selected site, company, machine, and date range. Choose portrait or landscape, then use Save as PDF in the preview.
